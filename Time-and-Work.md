# ⏱️ Time and Work — Short Tricks

## Core Concept

If A can do work in **n days**, A's 1-day work = **1/n**

## LCM Method (Fastest!)

> **Step:** Take LCM of all days as total work units.

### Example:
A does work in 4 days, B in 6 days. Together?

- LCM(4,6) = 12 (total units)
- A's rate = 12/4 = **3 units/day**
- B's rate = 12/6 = **2 units/day**
- Together = 5 units/day
- Time = 12/5 = **2.4 days** ✅

## Shortcuts

### Together Formula
```
Time together = (A × B) / (A + B)
```
> A=4, B=6 → (4×6)/(4+6) = 24/10 = **2.4 days**

### A is x times faster than B
If A is 3× faster than B, and B takes 12 days:
- A takes 12/3 = **4 days**
- Together: (3+1)/3 × B_time / (1+3) ... use LCM instead!

### Pipes and Cistern (same concept!)
- Filling pipe = positive work
- Draining pipe = negative work

> Pipe A fills in 6h, B drains in 12h. Together → 1/6 - 1/12 = 1/12 → **12 hours**

## Practice Problems

1. A takes 8 days, B takes 12 days. Together? → *4.8 days*
2. 5 workers finish in 10 days. 10 workers finish in? → *5 days*
